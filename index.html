About me

Hi, I'm Yuchen Zhang, a Sensory Science 2nd-year PhD candidate at the University of Nottingham. My PhD project is about consumer emotional responses towards sustainable foods. The first study of my PhD involves an online survey to investigate the impact of sharing environmental information on consumer perception of protein-based products, including plant-based burger, edible insect burger and lab-grown beef burger, as well as conventional beef burger as a control sample. The cross-cultral element will also involved in my second research.
